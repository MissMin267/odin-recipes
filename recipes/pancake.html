Pancake

Description

This is the recipe for the Oats Apple Pancake.You can replace apple with any other fruit of your liking. Maple syrup is optional and can be replaced by Vanilla syrup or cinnamon powder.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Oats
  • 1 Apple
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • Maple Syrup(optional)

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients to a blender & make into a paste
  2. Heat a pan & spread some unsalted butter on it.
  3. After the pan gets heated enough spread the batter & rotate the pancake so that it won't get burnt.
  4. Serve into a plate with maple syrup once the pancake gets completely cooked.
