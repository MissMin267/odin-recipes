Ice Popsicle

Description

This is the recipe for the summer favorite Ice Popsicles. You can either make this into fruit popsicles or coffee popsicles depending on your liking.

Ingredients

  • Fruit Puree of your choice
  • Milk(optional)
  • Chia Seeds(optional)

Steps

  1. Take some fruit puree of your choice.
  2. You can add some milk in this step but this is optional.
  3. Slightly blend the ingredients in a blender.
  4. Fill the cubes in the ice tray with the fruit blend and keep a wood holder.
  5. Keep them in the freezer and TADA! Your fruit popsicles will be ready in no time.
  6. You can replace fruit blend with coffee or fruit teas according to your liking.
  7. You can even top them with soaked chia seeds but this is optional.
